What I Do

I am a graphic designer with over a decade of professional experience in my field. Completely self taught, I built up my portfolio and managed to land a job as a Production Artist for an international nursing scrubs brands. I did get a double major in Advertsing and Marketing from Florida State University, but by the time I learned Graphic Design was a career, I was a semester away from graduation. I taught myself the basics over the years and managed to get a foot in when Uniform Advantage took a chance on me. From there, I had several other jobs before landing my dream job two years ago at the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention in Atlanta. I was going to relocate when I learned last year I had cancer. I am now in remission, but decided not to relocate after all. Luckily, I am able to work remotely as their Visual Information Specialist and occaisional Project Manager/Art Director across several campaigns and centers.
